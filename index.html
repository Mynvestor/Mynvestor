Common Questions

What are the fees for trading?

Online listed equity trades are commission-free, and there is no charge to work with a Mynvestor Financial Consultant. Other account fees or fund expenses may apply. Contact us for details.

How do I open an account?

Prepare the required documents and complete the online account opening process. Once established, fund your account and start investing. Start now.

How can I fund my account?

Fund your account via bank transfers, mobile money services, or check deposits. Full instructions are provided after account opening.

What about taxes for Malawian investors?

Malawian investors typically face no tax obligations on Malawi Stock Exchange trades, but consult your tax advisor for personalized advice.

What educational resources are available?

Access free workshops, webinars, and market insights to support your investment decisions. Visit our Learn section for more.

How secure is my information?

We use advanced security measures, including encryption and multi-factor authentication, to protect your data. Our Security Guarantee covers unauthorized activity losses.
